Ophis Swap for Safe

Ophis Swap is a Safe App for MEV-protected, batch-settled token swaps with fee rebates, run directly inside your Safe. Swaps settle on CoW Protocol's audited GPv2 contracts, so you keep self-custody and never approve a separate dapp.

To add it: open app.safe.global, go to Apps, choose "Add custom app", and enter https://safe.ophis.fi. Then swap straight from your Safe.
