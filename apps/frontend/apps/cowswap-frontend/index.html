Ophis: the intent-based DEX aggregator with MEV-protected swaps

Describe a trade in plain language and Ophis routes you to a pre-filled order. A competitive solver network fills it MEV-protected in a batch auction, and you sign with your own wallet, no custody, no front-running.

Built for the agent era: swap from an AI agent (MCP, GOAT, AgentKit), from a Safe, or via the embeddable widget and SDK, across 12 EVM chains: from Ethereum and Base to Optimism, Unichain, Arbitrum and BNB. Integrators and agents earn a rebate on every swap they route.
